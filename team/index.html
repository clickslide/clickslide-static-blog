Team

Gabriel Ortiz
ClickSlide Founder and CEO
Gabriel holds a Master’s Degree in Animation Design and attended the University of Edinburgh Informatics Department where he was an Entrepreneur in Residence. Before committing full-time to ClickSlide as CEO, Gabriel is best known in London for curating The London Startup Digest, building this weekly mailing list for the best technology events in London to over 4,000 subscribers in less than 10 months. He is also organizing a mobile-focused networking event held in Barcelona during the Mobile World Congress.

 

 

 

 

 

Aaron Franco

ClickSlide Founder and CTO
Aaron has 7 years’ experience with Object Oriented Programming (OOP) and 4 years’ experience building automated systems for Apple’s OS X and the WordPress platform. After living in Japan for 7 years and learning to speak Japanese fluently, he moved to London where he co-founded ClickSlide. As CTO of ClickSlide he guides the development of software that will revolutionize the way people use and develop cloud applications. He has spent the past 10 years researching and developing new technologies that he believes will be the future of “The Internet of Things.” The focus of his career has been turning theoretical technology into practical solutions in the form of mobile applications on and off the web.

 

Ian Morrison
ClickSlide Chairman
After a long and successful career in films and television, Ian became a Director of Rare Medium, Europe, which was formed from the merger in 1999 between the eponymous US Internet consultancy and Carlyle Media Group, the television and business communications group he founded in 1978 and of which he was Executive Chairman. He currently holds non-executive directorships in companies in the software development, mobile publishing, advertising and LED lighting fields. For seven years he was a director of Skillset, the national Sector Skills Council for creative media.

 

Michael Carter
ClickSlide CFO
After being awarded an Honours Degree in Business Studies from the University of Hertfordshire Michael qualified as a Chartered Accountant with KPMG in 1978. Since then he has held a variety of positions with both quoted and unquoted software and services companies, most frequently as Finance Director. In these positions he has dealt with every aspect of management across a wide range of businesses. As a non-executive director of a bank, he is FSA regulated. He has also written two business books for would-be entrepreneurs.
